Frequently asked questions

Is this QR code generator really free?
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. QRcraft is free forever for personal and commercial use.
Are my QR codes stored on your servers?
No. Everything happens in your browser using JavaScript. We never see, store, or transmit your content. You can even disconnect from the internet after loading the page and it will still work.
Do QR codes expire?
QR codes created with QRcraft never expire. Because they encode the data directly (not a short link to a server), they work forever as long as the destination (e.g., your website) is still online.
What is the best size to print a QR code?
For most print uses, generate at 512px and print at minimum 2×2 cm (0.8 inches). For larger prints like posters, download the SVG version which scales to any size without quality loss.
What does error correction level mean?
Error correction lets a QR code be scanned even if part of it is damaged or covered (e.g., by a logo). Higher levels (Q, H) are more resilient but create denser QR codes. Use H if you plan to add a logo overlay.
Can I use these QR codes for my business?
Absolutely. There are no restrictions on commercial use. Use them on menus, business cards, packaging, posters, or anywhere else for free.
